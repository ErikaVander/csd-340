hobbies

Fish

I suppose I am now a fish hobbyist. This
hobby is really fun and relaxing and gets
even more fun (and expensive) when your
fish start to have fry. I currently have one
baby bristlenose pleco (possibly more
hiding and along with three adolescent
plecos), and six angelfish fry. I have two
tanks but would soon like to get a third to
give my fry more room to grow.

Art

Art is something I have loved for a long time.
Before learning about how exciting making a
living as an artist can be, I actually wanted
to be one. I don't do art as often now but I
hope to continue to paint, draw, and sketch
here and there in the future.

Piano

This hobby is one my mom chose for me
and I am forever grateful. It is so awesome
to be able to sit down and play a few pieces
in the evenings to try reflect the sunset in my
music. I had a classical teacher that taught
me that one song can paint an infinite
number of pictures if played in different
ways. She always asked me what picture I
saw when I played a song.
